FOOD CHAIN OF A TIGER||
The food chain of a tiger typically involves it hunting and consuming herbivores like deer or wild boar,establishing a predatot-prey relationship in its ecosystem.


   

LIFE CYCLE OF A TIGER||
Tigers progress from cubs reliant on their mothers to independent adults, facing threats like poaching, with an average lifespan of 10 to 15 years in the wild.


   

ANATOMY OF A TIGER||
The anatomy of a tiger includes powerful limbs, sharp claws, a robust skull, and distinctive striped fur for camouflage.


   

DIGESTIVE SYSTEM OF A TIGER||
The tiger's digestive system features a simple stomach and efficient small intestine for processing a carnivorous diet and extracting essential nutrients.
